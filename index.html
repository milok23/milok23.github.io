Rutgers Camden Before Urban Renewal

Rutgers Camden Before
Urban Renewal

A historical investigation

Before the Rutgers University–Camden community inhabited its current location, the area was filled with residents, businesses, and an active and diverse community. This project aims to recover the history of the site of Rutgers–Camden prior to the urban renewal demolition of an earlier neighborhood in the 1960s and to raise awareness of that history. Specifically, this project dives into the lives of the people and places who lived in that neighborhood from 1880 until urban renewal occurred in the 1960s.
